# Bill Of Materials

`BOM.xlsx` is the original purchase record. This page is the short public build summary, with three user-supplied additions listed separately.

## Core Electronics

- ESP32 dev board: main controller.
- TMC2209 stepper driver: conveyor motor driver.
- NEMA17 stepper motor: conveyor drive.
- MG995/MG996-class servo: rotary chute selector.
- TCS3200/GY-31 color sensor: color classification.
- IR break-beam pairs: size classification.
- Waveshare 2 inch Mini LCD: operator display.
- LM2596 buck converter: 5V rail.
- Inline fuse holder: LiPo input protection.
- 4700uF electrolytic capacitor: stepper driver input bulk capacitance.

## Mechanical And Build Parts

- LEGO brick set: 24-brick sorting set.
- Wood frame stock: main structure.
- GT2 belt and pulleys: conveyor drive.
- MR85ZZ and 608ZZ bearings: conveyor shafts and rollers.
- Printed brackets, shroud, chute, and guides: machine-specific geometry.
- UHMW or PTFE tape: low-friction chute surface.
- Black foam and electrical tape: light blocking and bounce control.
- M3 hardware: printed part mounting.
- Cable ties and braided sleeve: cable routing.

## User-Supplied Additions

These parts were ordered outside `BOM.xlsx` and are included because they are part of the finished build.

- XT60 LiPo pigtails: battery connection.
- 5-port WAGO connectors: power distribution.
- Wood screws: frame and bracket fastening.
